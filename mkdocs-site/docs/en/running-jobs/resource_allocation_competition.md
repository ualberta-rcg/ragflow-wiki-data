The Resource Allocation Competition (RAC) enables faculty members and their research groups to access compute, storage and cloud resources beyond what can be obtained via the [Rapid Access Service](../policies/rapid_access_service.md).

!!! note
    If you are not sure about which application process is best for your project, please email [allocations@tech.alliancecan.ca](mailto:allocations@tech.alliancecan.ca).

You may also wish to consult the RAC [Frequently Asked Questions](../policies/rac_faq.md) and [Technical Glossary](https://docs.computecanada.ca/wiki/Technical_glossary_for_the_resource_allocation_competitions).
