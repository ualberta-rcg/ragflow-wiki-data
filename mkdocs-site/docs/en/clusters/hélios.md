!!! warning "Retirement of Hélios"
    Hélios has been decommissioned.

Hélios was a supercomputer with general purpose graphics processor nodes (GPGPU). It was installed in Laval University's computing centre in the spring of 2014. In prevision for its decommissioning, interactive access through SSH was blocked on October 26th, 2022. Data can still be retrieved via Globus.
